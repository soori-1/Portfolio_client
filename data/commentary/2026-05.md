## Portfolio Highlights – May 2026

- The Strait of Hormuz crisis defined market direction, producing a sharp drawdown followed by an equally sharp recovery to near-record highs.
- Emerging markets outperformed developed markets globally, and the portfolio's EM allocation (EEM, EWZ, EWT, MCHI) captured this rotation effectively.
- Polish equities (EPOL) were the single largest contributor at the position level, supported by EU defence spending acceleration, NATO commitment uplift, and EUR strength.
- Brazil (EWZ) and Taiwan (EWT) added further alpha through Selic rate cuts and TSMC-driven semiconductor demand respectively.
- European exposure remained the principal area of weakness, reflecting the region's energy vulnerability and limited technology participation.
- The portfolio's diversified construction allowed both defensive hedges and growth-oriented positions to contribute simultaneously.
